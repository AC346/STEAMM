About US

Hello we are the Pterodactyls.
To show the range of jobs in S.T.E.A.M and place that information on a source wheere people of all ages can access it

From guest speakers to doing research on our own, we have discovered that jobs in S.T.E.A.M are increasing everyday,
however there aren't as many people as needed in those fields.
Maybe it is because people want to take the safe route as doctors and lawyers,
or they just do ont know the value they will gain from being in S.T.E.A.M.

This website is a great opportunity for people to learn and hopefully develop a want for being in S.T.E.A.M.

We also want to show the wide range of the people who work in these fields. Peope with disbabilties are often thought of as people who won't get places or that will need help from people to "make it". However with our Unique people tab, we show that people with disabilties have done great things in this field and has "made it" based on their determination and passion for what they want to do.
