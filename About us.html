About US

Hello we are the Pterodactyls.

MISSION: To show the range of jobs in S.T.E.A.M and place that information on a source where people of all ages can access it

DISCOVERY: Jobs in S.T.E.A.M are increasing everyday, however, there aren't alot of people who has access to those jobs in those fields.

PURPOSE OF WEBSITE: To expose people to the great opportunities that are available in S.T.E.A.M. Also to show the wide range of those people who work in those fields-- including those who are disabled. People with disabilities are often looked as those who aren't able to do much and that's why we wanted to incorporate them into our page. We did this because we don't want others in their situation to feel discouraged. People with disabilities have done and will continue doing GREAT things, so, you can to!
