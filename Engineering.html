Engineering

Mechanical Engineer: Design power producing machines with the application of
physics, engineering etc.

Qualifications: At least a Bachelor’s degree in mechanical engineering.



Software Engineer: Analyzes user needs, provides consulting and coordination of software installation.

Qualifications: Have a bachelor's degree in computer science, programming, mathematics, or a related field. Master a variety of programming languages, such as C++, C#, JavaScript, and Python and practice building software.



Civil Engineer: An engineer who designs and maintains roads,bridges,dams etc.

Qualifications: Need a Bachelor’s Degree in civil engineering and a graduate degree and license for promotion to senior positions.
